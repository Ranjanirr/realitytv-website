Dating

The Bachelor

What happens when a man wants to marry, and suddenly he is thrown into a room with 30 female suitors? Watch him take a month to choose his perfect, and the girls going through turbulent emotions as they anticipate being “the one”.

My 90 Day Fiance

Finding love in a foreign country can be exciting, yet stressful. 90 day Fiance explores the positive connections and conflicts as two people have only 90 days to decide if they want to marry.. Not only do they have to deal with eachother, but also their family demands.

Survival

Life Below zero

Is surviving January hard for you? Imagine that month’s weather in SUMMER and January another level. Yep. That’s what the cast of Life Below Zero go through in tundra Alaska. With very little industry these people’s survival are in their own hands.

Survivor

Imagine being thrown on an island with only the island and a bunch of people to help (or hurt) you. On top of that, you have to worry about getting eliminated if you lose one of the silly challences they throw at you. If you want to learn to survive in a super real world, this is the best show.
