What it is used for

Are you tired of watching fiction? You may have considered Reality TV, but have heard mixed opinions about the shows out there. If you are not sure where to get started, here is the guide! Just click on the “shows” tab, and you will see a directory of all reality shows out there based on genre!

Why it was founded:

The founder, Ranjani Ramanathan, was fond of Reality TV ever since she was a teenager. She knows that real life has more suspense and drama than even the most exciting fiction. However, she noticed that other people were reluctant to watch they cannot make sense from what they hear from other people. To encourage people to watch reality TV, she made a site, right here,where people can browse reality TV shows by their topic of interest!
